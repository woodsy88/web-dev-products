About The Event

Once Upon a Murder is a night filled with mystery, celebration, suspense and is all for a charitable cause! Be apart of an interactive live murder mystery game set in 1899 New Orleans. Experience endless entertainment, music, food & drinks, interactive performers, prizes, games, and a little bit of Mardi Gras History. This event is 19+ and a semi-formal dress code is in effect.

Story

The story takes places in New Orleans in 1899 when the first female Krewe (secret society) was formed called the Calypso. They secretly financed the biggest Mardi Gras Masquerade Ball ever and invited the oldest male society the Sinis Krewe to attend. Shortly into the night of celebration a Sinis krewe member is found DEAD. Now it is time to find out who at the ball is the Masked MURDERER?

Murder Mystery

You will be a key player in solving the Mardi Gras Murder Mystery. Engage with our 9 suspects (live actors), other guests, and decor to find clues and solve the crime. Correct accusations will be entered into a pool for the grand prize, and winners will be drawn live!

Entertainment

Enjoy a wealth of Mardi Gras themed entertainment while you’re working on solving the mystery. Take fun and quirky photos in our Mugshot Photo Booth, enjoy magic tricks from our wandering magician, have your tarot cards drawn by our New Orlean’s Voodoo Queen if you dare and witness the magic array of spinning lights during our live LED twirling performance!

Food & Beverage

Indulge in a variety of great food and tasty drinks. We have selected an assortment of gourmet hors d'oeuvres that will hit the spot and our cash bar will have a selection of spirits, wine and beer to get the party going.

Win Big

There are tons of amazing prizes to win at our event including the grand prize for solving the murder mystery, giveaways and a silent auction loaded with awesome stuff to bid on.

Charity

All the proceeds from Once Upon a Murder will go to the Covenant House Charity Foundation, and George Brown College Scholarships and Bursaries. Your participation will go towards something GREAT!.

PARTNERS

Proceeds go to the Covenant House

Covenant House is Canada’s largest homeless youth agency. They work to aid suffering children who are homeless by providing a range of services under one roof. The Covenant House provides kids in crisis with basic human needs immediately, sanctuary, values, structure and choice. The Covenant House is involved with suffering youth in many different ways; they have a crisis shelter, a job centre, an onsite high school, and offer runaway prevention presentations, job training programs, and mental health programs. The Covenant House plays a large role in the community and offers many services to help those who are in need.

Featured story by Shot in The Dark Mysteries

Shot in The Dark Mysteries is a live interactive game company based out of Toronto and serves clients worldwide through their online website. Shot in The Dark Mysteries caters to various types of parties, from large events to small dinner parties, they can create stories that will sure fit your event. Leigh Clements also known as “The Mystery Maiden” started this company in Calgary in 2007 with the goal of creating unforgettable memories for all her guests. Eliminating stressful, embarrassing, and time-consuming aspects of a traditional murder mystery game, she helps hosts and hostesses create an event they are proud of and that guests truly enjoy.
